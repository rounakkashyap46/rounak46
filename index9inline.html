ABOUT INDIAN FLAG

The Indian national flag, also known as the Tiranga (meaning "three colours") , is a horizontal tricolor with India saffron (Kesari) at the top, white in the middle, and India green at the bottom. In the center of the white band is a navy-blue wheel with 24 spokes, called the Ashoka Chakra. The flag's design was adopted by the Constituent Assembly of India on July 22, 1947.
