INDIAN FLAG

How the Indian Flag can Inspire your Work ValuesThe Indian national flag, also known as the Tiranga (meaning "three colours"), is a horizontal tricolor with saffron (Kesari) at the top, white in the middle, and India green at the bottom. In the center of the white band is a navy-blue wheel with 24 spokes, called the Ashoka Chakra.
    • Here's a more detailed look at the flag:
      Colors and their symbolism:
      Saffron: Represents courage, sacrifice, and renunciation.
      White: Represents peace, truth, and purity.
      Green: Represents fertility, growth, and auspiciousness.
      Ashoka Chakra: The wheel symbolizes righteousness, progress, and the Dharma.

    INDIAN VOLLEY TEAM

    The Indian volleyball team has had a rollercoaster ride in its history. Although they have never qualified for the Olympics, they have appeared in the World Championship twice. Despite having little to no presence at the world stage in its history, the team has enjoyed some success at the continental level by clinching a silver medal and two bronze medals at the Asian Games. After the decline post 1980s, the team has fared well at the recently introduced Asian Cup competition, receiving a silver and a bronze medal so far. CLICK HERE
